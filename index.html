About Jaffna

Jaffna is the capital city of the Northern Province of Sri Lanka. Known for its majestic historic forts, unique cuisine, vibrant colorful temples, and warm hospitality, it stands as an unmatched destination for heritage travel explorers.

Popular Destination Highlights

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil

An iconic historic Hindu temple complex characterized by beautiful golden arches and vibrant spiritual architecture.

Jaffna Fort

Built by the Portuguese in 1618, this monumental stronghold overlooks the scenic lagoon landscape.

Casuarina Beach

Famous for its shallow calm waters, white sands, and rows of pristine casuarina trees.
